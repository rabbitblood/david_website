Theme For This Project

the theme for this project is for a friend's photography profile website

Prime Objectives Of the Website

the goal of building this website is for people to see his photography online, and promote himself. there will be a contact section where you can send email to him, so he will have better chance to get job, or even better if some people wants to buy his photography.

Detailed Analysis Of The Ideal Audience

I believe the audience of this website will be artist or prople who is looking for an artist. Language and gender, or anything else shouldnt really be a problem. There wont be photo selling section since he is not fully professional into photography yet.

Description of the information architecture

the navigation should be simple, there is the main page for general infomation, a photography page to put on all the best photography he has shoot for presenting, a about me page for information only about himself, and a contact page for giving out a way to contact with him.
